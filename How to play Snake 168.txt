HOW TO PLAY SNAKE 168

To Start The Game:
 - Press the Start Game Button

 Controls:
  - Player 1
  	 - W, A, S, D to move the RED snake
  - Player 2
     - Left, Up, Down, Right on the arrow keys to move the BLUE snake

 Objective:
  - Collect as many food pellets as you can!
  - Don't die to the walls or other snakes!

 How to Win:
  - Have the other snake run into you!
  - Survive until the other snake hits the wall!
